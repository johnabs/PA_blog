Contact Me

If you have questions, comments, concerns, or other nearly alliterative things you want to send my way, my email is johnbs1234@gmail.com. However, there are a few ground rules:

  • Do not email me about product reviews. As much as I would love to make some money off my blog, I’m not comfortable risking my (albeit small) reputation for cash. I do take donations though, if you want to support my work.
  • If you want me to review some FLOSS software or service, I would likely be more okay with it. Maybe.
  • If you want to debate ideas I’m down for it, but let’s try to keep things civil.
  • Please keep things brief. I know that’s hypocritical, but the more time I spend on email, the less I can spend living.

For any code I write or have edited/changed myself, my github is https://github.com/johnabs. Feel free to contribute if you want, or send suggestions.
